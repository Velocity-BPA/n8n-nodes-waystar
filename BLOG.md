# Automate Healthcare Revenue Cycle Management with the New Waystar Node for n8n

We're excited to announce the release of our latest community node: n8n-nodes-waystar! Created by Velocity BPA, this powerful integration brings Waystar's comprehensive healthcare Revenue Cycle Management (RCM) capabilities directly into your n8n workflows.

## The Healthcare Automation Challenge

Healthcare providers and billing companies face enormous administrative burdens managing patient eligibility verification, claims submission, remittance processing, and denial management. These critical processes are often fragmented across multiple systems, leading to delays, errors, and lost revenue. Manual data entry between systems wastes countless hours that could be spent on patient care or strategic initiatives.

## Streamline Your RCM Workflow

The Waystar node for n8n eliminates these bottlenecks by connecting your existing systems directly to Waystar's industry-leading RCM platform. Now you can build automated workflows that handle eligibility checks, submit and track claims, process remittance data, and manage denials—all without writing complex code or toggling between multiple applications.

## Key Features

Our Waystar node supports the essential operations healthcare organizations need:

- **Eligibility Verification**: Automatically check patient insurance eligibility before appointments or procedures
- **Claims Management**: Submit, track, and manage claims programmatically
- **Remittance Processing**: Process and reconcile payment data efficiently
- **Denial Management**: Track, analyze, and respond to claim denials systematically

Whether you're integrating with your EHR system, building automated reporting dashboards, or creating complex multi-step billing workflows, this node provides the flexibility to design solutions that match your unique processes.

## Getting Started

Installing the Waystar node is simple. In your n8n instance, run:


npm install n8n-nodes-waystar


After installation, restart n8n and you'll find the Waystar node available in your node palette. Connect it with your Waystar credentials and start building workflows immediately.
